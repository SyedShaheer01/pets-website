Untold Story


In the summer of 1962, a courageous dog named Daboo became a symbol of unwavering loyalty and sacrifice. On a stormy afternoon, a young girl found herself in peril when she unknowingly stepped near a fallen live electrical wire on the wet ground. Sensing the imminent danger, Daboo, her faithful companion, sprang into action. Barking urgently to warn her, he dashed forward and pushed her out of harm’s way with his sturdy body. The girl was saved, but in his selfless act, Daboo came into contact with the live wire and tragically lost his life to electrocution. Daboo’s heroic sacrifice left an indelible mark on the hearts of all who heard his story, a timeless reminder of the boundless love and bravery that animals so often show to protect the ones they cherish


To honor the incredible bravery of Daboo, the loyal dog who sacrificed his life to save a young girl in 1962, a heartfelt movement has been created to keep his memory alive. The initiative celebrates Daboo’s unwavering loyalty and courage while promoting causes that align with his heroic spirit, such as animal rescue efforts, shelter funding, and pet safety awareness. Through storytelling, community events, and outreach programs, Daboo’s legacy serves as a reminder of the selfless love that animals offer to humans. By sharing his story and supporting these meaningful causes, the memory of Daboo continues to inspire kindness and compassion, ensuring his heroic act is never forgotten.
